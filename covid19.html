Covid-19 corona vaccine process

online registration

First you have to complete the online registration by verifying your national identity card and correct mobile number through this portal.

SMS Notification

After registering online, you will receive a text message on your mobile phone stating the date of vaccination and the name of the center.

Vaccination at the vaccination center

Subject to receiving the text message on the mobile phone, Ticacard will appear in person at the immunization center on the specified date along with the national identity card and consent form and receive the Covid-19 vaccine.
